Thank you for installing YouTube No Disturbance.

See my recommendations below for the best results when using this extension

  1. Reload any running YouTube pages when you make a change in your options
  2. Enable the extension to "Always Allow on youtube.com"
    1. Navigate to youtube.com
    2. Right click YouTube No Disturbance's icon on your extension toolbar
    3. Enable "Always Allow on youtube.com"
    4. Reload any running YouTube pages
